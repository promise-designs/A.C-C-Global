About Us

Welcome to A.C C Global Solar Energy

A.C C Global Solar Energy is a trusted and registered dealer in all kinds of solar energy systems, proudly located at Shop F1485B, Alaba International Market, Ojo, Lagos State, Nigeria. For years, we have been at the forefront of providing reliable, affordable, and high-quality solar solutions to homes, businesses, and industries across the country.

Our mission is simple: to make clean, sustainable energy accessible to every Nigerian. We don't just sell products — we provide complete energy solutions. From helping you choose the right solar panel size to professional installation and ongoing maintenance, our experienced team is with you every step of the way. We also deliver nationwide, so no matter where you are in Nigeria, we've got you covered.
