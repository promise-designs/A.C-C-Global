What We Fix

Common Solar Issues We Solve

Panel Issues

Dirt buildup, cracks, shading problems, and reduced output. We clean, repair, or replace panels as needed.

Inverter Problems

No output, error codes, overheating, or complete failure. We repair or replace inverters with quality units.

Battery Failures

Swollen batteries, short backup time, or complete death. We test and replace with premium batteries.

Wiring & Connections

Loose connections, burnt terminals, or faulty wiring. We inspect and rewire for safety and efficiency.

Charge Controllers

Overcharging, undercharging, or controller failure. We calibrate or replace MPPT/PWM controllers.

Performance Audit

Full system analysis to identify why your energy output has dropped and how to restore it.
