Services

Our Services

Solar Panel Installation

Professional installation of high-efficiency monocrystalline and polycrystalline solar panels for homes, offices, and industrial buildings. We assess your energy needs and design a system that maximizes power output.

Solar Battery Sales

We stock premium deep-cycle solar batteries including tubular, lithium-ion, and gel batteries from trusted brands. All batteries come with warranty and are tested for maximum performance and longevity.

Inverter & Charge Controller

Sales and installation of pure sine wave inverters and MPPT/PWM charge controllers. We match the right inverter capacity to your load requirements for seamless power backup.

Solar Freezer & Generator

Energy-efficient solar-powered freezers and portable solar generators perfect for homes, shops, and outdoor use. Zero fuel cost, zero noise, pure solar power.

System Maintenance & Repair

Comprehensive maintenance services including panel cleaning, battery health checks, inverter troubleshooting, and wiring inspections. We keep your solar system running at peak efficiency year-round.

Nationwide Delivery

We deliver solar products to every state in Nigeria — Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, Kano, Enugu, and beyond. Secure packaging, reliable logistics partners, and real-time tracking on request.

Energy Consultation

Free energy audits and load analysis to determine the perfect solar system size for your needs. We calculate your daily consumption and recommend the most cost-effective setup.

After-Sales Support

Our relationship doesn't end at sale. We provide ongoing technical support, warranty claims assistance, and upgrade recommendations. Call or WhatsApp us anytime — we're here for you.
